The 'Stay Safe Online' Checklist:-

The digital landscape for Indian students is more connected than ever, but it also comes with sophisticated risks like AI-generated phishing and UPI "collect request" scams. Use this checklist to audit your digital habits and stay one step ahead of cybercriminals.

Stay Safe Online Checklist:-

Financial and UPI Safety:-

1)Never Enter PIN to Receive Money. 

2)Verify Collect Requests.

3)Check the Merchant Name After Scanning.

Device and App Security:-

1)Use Official App Stores Only.

2)Enable Biometric/App Lock. 

3)Disable Auto-Join for Public Wi-Fi.

Personal Data and Social Media:-

1)Audit Your Social Privacy. 

2)Spot AI Phishing.

If You Are Scammed: Act Within the Golden Hour

Reporting financial fraud within the first 60 minutes significantly increases the chances of the bank freezing the money before the scammer can withdraw it.

Channel:-                Contact Information

National Helpline:-      Call 1930 (Available 24/7)

Official Portal:-        cybercrime.gov.in

Action Step:-            Save screenshots of the chat, transaction IDs, and the scammer's number/UPI ID immediately.
